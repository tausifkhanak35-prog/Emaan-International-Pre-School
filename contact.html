Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups does Emaan International Pre-School accept?+

We accept children from 1.5 years to 5.5 years across four classes — Playgroup (1.5–2.5 yrs), Nursery (2.5–3.5 yrs), LKG (3.5–4.5 yrs) and UKG (4.5–5.5 yrs). Each class is carefully designed for the developmental stage of your child.

Where exactly is the school located in Mysore?+

We are located at Building No. 610, North East of NR Mohalla, 3rd B' Cross, 2nd Stage, R.S.Naidu Nagar, Mysuru, Karnataka – 570007. You can open us in Google Maps or WhatsApp us for exact directions and we will guide you immediately!

What documents are required for admission?+

You will need the child's birth certificate (photocopy), 4 passport-size photos of the child, parent's Aadhar card (photocopy), previous school records if applicable, and vaccination or medical records. Our team will guide you through the complete process after your initial enquiry.

Does Emaan follow the Montessori method?+

Yes! We are Montessori-inspired — our classrooms are prepared environments, children learn through hands-on materials, and education is child-led and child-paced. We combine this beautifully with structured academic learning, Islamic values and 12+ enrichment courses for a complete education.

Are Islamic studies part of the daily curriculum?+

Absolutely! Islamic values are at the heart of everything we do. We have a dedicated Hifz Course for Quran memorization with proper Tajweed, and an Adab Course teaching beautiful Islamic manners of daily life. Islamic studies, daily duas and moral character building are woven into every aspect of our school day.

How do I enroll my child?+

The easiest way is to fill our Admission Form on the Admissions page and tap "Send on WhatsApp" — your enquiry reaches us instantly! You can also directly WhatsApp us at +91 77955 57292, call us, or visit our school in person. We will guide you through the entire process warmly and quickly, In Sha Allah.

Is the school safe and secure for young children?+

Your child's safety is our absolute top priority. Our campus has biometric access-controlled doors, full CCTV surveillance across all areas, constant staff supervision, child-friendly safe infrastructure, regular medical check-ups and strict hygiene protocols maintained daily. Parents can drop their children with complete confidence and peace of mind.
