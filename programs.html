Faith & Character

Islamic Education Courses

At Emaan, Islamic values are not just taught — they are lived and breathed every day. Our dedicated Islamic courses build children of faith, character and beautiful Islamic conduct.

📖

Hifz Course

A beautifully structured program introducing children to Quran memorization with proper tajweed rules. We build a deep, joyful and lifelong love for the Book of Allah through gentle repetition and encouragement.

  • Structured Quran memorization program
  • Proper Tajweed rules taught from the start
  • Short Surahs memorized with correct pronunciation
  • Building a lifelong connection with the Quran
  • Joyful, encouraging and patient teaching style
🌙

Adab Course

The Adab course teaches the beautiful Islamic manners and etiquette of daily life. From greeting with Salaam to showing kindness, gratitude and respect — these lessons form the foundation of a child's moral character.

  • Greeting with Assalamu Alaikum always
  • Respect for elders, parents and teachers
  • Sharing, kindness and generosity in action
  • Daily duas and Islamic daily routines
  • Building a strong, righteous Muslim character
